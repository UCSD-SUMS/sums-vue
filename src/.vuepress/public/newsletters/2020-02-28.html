Interview a Professor with Dr. Brandon Seward

In the "Interview with a Professor" event, a UCSD Department of Mathematics faculty member will be interviewed while the audience will have the chance to ask questions near the end of the event. The discussion will give attendees the opportunity to interact with and learn from a faculty member in a different setting than usual, i.e., in lectures or office hours. Topics to be covered may include but is not limited to the member's teaching, research, and academic/career path and experience.This quarter, we will be interviewing one of the newest additions to the UCSD Math Department, Professor Brandon Seward. Professor Seward is interested in Ergodic theory, topological dynamics, descriptive set theory, and group theory. He received his PhD in mathematics at the University of Michigan and was a postdoctoral fellow at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. If you are interested in learning more about the professor, his area of research, and/or his academic path, come to our event!
