SUMS Staff Application Form 

It's an exciting time of the year. We're going to be selecting SUMS staff members for the following academic year! This is the application for becoming an officer for the 2019-2020 academic year. Think of a staff member as being one level below an officer but one level above a member.

Some of the benefits of becoming a staff member are that you are getting the experience needed if you're interested in running for an officer position, and you also get the opportunity to create a community with the officers of SUMS. If you're interested in becoming a staff member, fill in the application below by Sunday, March 29th at 11:59 p.m.

SUMS Member Application Form 

If you are interested in becoming a member of the Society of Undergraduate Mathematics Students and have met the membership requirements listed below, please complete the form below. Please complete this application by Sunday, March 29th, 2020. 

Here are the benefits you may receive as a member:
  • Eligibility to run for office.
  • A vote in elections.
  • A vote in votes opened to members.
  • Invitation to team building events.
  • Discounts, including, but not limited to: UCSD mathematics graduation stoles, and SUMS merchandise.
  • Membership until graduation.
Requirements for becoming a member shall be:
  • Must be a registered UCSD undergraduate student (of any major).
  • Attend (and sign in to) 2 (communicated in advance of each academic quarter) of SUMS events (includes GBM).
  • Attend (and sign in to) 3 (communicated in advance of each academic quarter) of SUMS board meetings.
